BOLLYWOOD DANCE Grades K-4
  • Location Small Gym
  • Pickup Time 4:15 pm
  • Pickup Location Gym
Bollywood dance is a contemporary Indian dance that combines the dance styles of classical Indian dance, belly dance, jazz and hip-hop with a little bit of mystique and a lot of foot-tapping music. It's a great way to experience a holistic physical activity, broaden cultural experiences and develop musical skills. Dancers are taught skillful hand movements and facial expressions to convey emotions and the story behind the music, all the while havingfun and enjoying a cultural phenomenon that has swept dance studios worldwide. Dancers will learn specific choreography to the popular Indian songs and an exciting recital for parents on the last day of the class. Session fee includes a costume rental for recital, provided by instructors from Xplore Cultures. $116 • Jan 25-Mar 14 (8 Classes) • min/max: 5/20
