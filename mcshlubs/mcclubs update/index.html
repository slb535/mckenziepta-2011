Registration is open through 2 pm, October 5

In addition to all the favorite clubs returning, we are pleased to introduce six new clubs that we hope your children will enjoy: Drum Club, Bollywood Dance, Amazing Minds, Smart Board, Breakfast & Books and My Gym.  Click here for detailed descriptions of the clubs as well as dates/times and eligible grades.

Please note that the process of registering for McClubs is getting a make-over this year, making it both easier and more efficient. All registration and payments will take place on-line.  In the September 24th Monitor you will find a registration link or you can come right back here to register.

Class rosters with club details will be emailed to parents between Oct 7-10.

So remember...

  • Registration is now open and closes at 2 pm, October 5.
  • New process -- online registration only.
  • Fall Clubs begin on Friday, October 8. Please refer to each club's description for specific beginning, end and skipped dates.
  • Morning clubs are from 8-8:45 am. Afternoon clubs are from 3:20-4:15pm.

Contact mcclubs@mckenziepta.com with any questions.
