Share What You Can Do

Ushers
Want a V I P seat for the Variety Show? We need volunteers to help usher for the Variety Show. The job is easy...come 30 minutes before the show and help man the crowd. You will then get a reserved seat for you and your immediate family only. Spots are limited! Contact Deanna Goldman.

Go behind the scenes
We’re always looking for creative talent to help complete sets or even costumes for the songs. Any amount of time helps. We usually need help beginning in January. Contact 2011 Show Director Wendy Parris if you can help.

Act before the show
Things really start moving for the show in mid to late February. That’s when we begin ticket sales, T-shirt sales, DVD sales. If you can help for a morning, we could really use your expertise. Contact 2011 Show Director Wendy Parris.

For older kids
 Before each performance we sell pretzels and water to the throng queued up in front of the auditorium. We could use your responsible kids to handle the sales – and, yes, even promote those pretzels. Because all the money collected goes to the PTA! If you have an interested (and, yes, interesting) kid, please contact 2011 Show Finance Dude Glenn Warning.
