PTA Communications at McKenzie

The McKenzie PTA communications team is here to help you get the word out about all your events.  When planning your event, don’t forget that its success depends in part on how well you communicate.  Although several different communication methods are available, the primary tools are:

  • the McKenzie PTA Website, and
  • the McKenzie Monitor (email newsletter)

Submitting a Communications Request:  New this year – almost all communication requests can be submitted via the McKenzie PTA Website. Click below to fill out and submit your communication request:

Communication Submission

If you have general questions, comments or your request doesn’t work with this form, send an email to the McKenzie PTA general email: McKenziepta@yahoo.com.  See below for additional instructions and information for your communications requests.

What’s Available: The McKenzie PTA Website and McKenzie Monitor should always be your focus, but other options are available.  The front hallway bulletin board is available for kid-focused events.  Note that this bulletin board space will be a bit smaller than last year to allow for space for student art.  You may also submit materials for the Wilmette Life (Pioneer Press).  Paper flyers using Backpack Express are only available under very limited circumstances and will require a special approval process, so please consider paperless alternatives first.  Note that there are also bulletin boards in the front and back entryways (3 of them) for reminders, community information and sign up sheets.

Publicity: Need extra help figuring out how best to communicate?  Good news, Erin Lyons has taken on a new role to help committees with publicity.  She can help with communications timing, strategy, look and feel, etc. for your event.  Already have someone on your committee in charge of communications?  Feel free to consult with Erin on any questions you might have.  We’ll also be posting some planning tools on the McKenzie PTA website that might help.

We’re looking forward to a great year.  If you have any questions or suggestions, please email or call any of us on the Communications team:

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION FOR COMMUNICATIONS SUBMISSIONS:

Website Requests:

  • Submit all requests at least one week prior to desired posting.
  • The website landing page has limited real estate and sometimes the timing of various events will mean not everyone will get a spot on the landing page.  We will do our best to accommodate all requests, but keep in mind that the sooner we know about the request the easier it will be to meet.
  • If you have a lot of information to communicate, you should split up your message into a short piece for the landing page, with a link to a separate page. Every committee has its own page and a link can be placed on the landing page to go to another, dedicated page. 
  • Request should be submitted in ready-to-use form.  In other words, it should contain all the required detail without need for significant editing.  We need to rely on your submission for all the facts, so please make sure you validate the details with your PTA VP or committee chair before submission.  If you need help putting together your communications material, please contact Erin Lyons.
  • Requests that include using PayPal need to be submitted 1 week prior to posting.

McKenzie Monitor Requests:

  • Submissions for each week’s Monitor must be in by 11:00 AM on Thursday of each week.
  • If you have a lot of information to communicate, consider splitting up your message into a short message for the Monitor with a link to the website.
  • Request should be submitted in ready-to-use form.  In other words, it should contain all the required detail without need for significant editing. We need to rely on your submission for all the facts, so please make sure you validate the details with your PTA VP or committee chair before submission.  If you need help putting together your communications material, please contact Erin Lyons.

Other Email Requests:

  • Sometimes an email reminder to the full McKenzie distribution is needed when events have changed, something falls on a weekend, or other issues arise.  As these are not the norm, requests don’t go through the standard request form; instead please send those requests to the McKenzie PTA mailbox or directly to Deb Sokol.

Bulletin Board Requests: 

  • This year, the space on the Main Lobby bulletin board is going to be a bit more limited.  This bulletin board will be available mainly for those events that are kid-focused – to get the students excited about the event.
  • Should you wish to use the Main Lobby bulletin board for your event, submit your request to the Mckenzie PTA mailbox approximately 1 month prior to when you’d like to put up the bulletin board with an explanation of what you are planning.
  • There are also bulletin boards in the front and back entryways (3 of them) for reminders, community information and sign up sheets.  Requests for posting on these boards, with 3 copies of the posting (for each board) are to be submitted directly to Dr. Welter at least 48 hours prior to posting.

Paper Flyers/Backpack Express: 

  • As the school continues to “Go Green”, Paper Flyers distributed via backpack express should almost never be needed.  Flyers or the content of a flyer can be posted on the website, and information can also be placed in the Monitor. 
  • In the rare event that a paper flyer is needed, you will need to fill out a Backpack Express Request form approximately 2 weeks before distribution, which will need to go to your PTA VP contact, who will send it to Dr. Welter for approval. 
  • You can find the Backpack Express Request form on the website at http://mckenziepta.com/communication.html.
