Celebrate Your Child’s Birthday with the
McKenzie Birthday Book Club!

I’ll bet you and your child:
- Love books
- Enjoy making birthdays special
- Want to help the school library grow

If your answer is “Yes,” I hope you’ll consider making your child’s birthday special by donating the cost of a new book ($20 on average) to the McKenzie Library Media Center. Your child can make a selection from our newest books that have been specially selected by Ms. Schoenberg, our librarian. Your child will get to be the first to check the book out. It will include a bookplate with your child’s name, birthday and the year the book was donated. This is a wonderful way to show your child the value you place on reading and education.

Paypal is closed for this committee until the 2011/12 school year

To pay by check, click here to download the birthday book order form.

Complete and return the form with a check made payable to McKenzie PTA. Checks can be dropped off in the PTA Birthday Book Club mailbox in the school office.

Thank you for your continued support of our school library.
