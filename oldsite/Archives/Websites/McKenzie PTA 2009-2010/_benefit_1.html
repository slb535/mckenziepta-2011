The Growing Minds Benefit committee would like to thank the entire McKenzie community for embracing the positive spirit that grew throughout all aspects of the Growing Minds Benefit.  We had 320 participants RSVP to the event.  All faculty attendees were sponsored by generous McKenzie parents (thank you!).  Accolades to everyone who participated in the underwriting opportunity via The Bottle Shop.  We raised more funds via the 2 Oprah Ticket & Gift Card Raffle than EVER before ($ 3,350).  Congrats to Raffle Winner:  Cara Gaziano.  The exquisite auction items most generously donated and actively bid on raised in excess of $ 34k.  A detailed accounting of benefit proceeds will be provided at the PTA meeting on 3/9. Did you know that the McKenzie Student Council crafted the whimsical floral pens used for bidding? After the bidding ended there was a packed floor of revelers dancing to our very own McKenzie-dad Band, Pop Rocks.  The evening was a tremendous success on so many levels.  As your Growing Minds Benefit co-chairs we thank you and appreciate all of your support – Kimberly Alcantara and Julie Farina. 

Growing Minds Benefit outstanding items:  Winners report (contact Kimberly or Julie with questions).  Double check expiration dates and fine print on items you won.  Turn in ASAP all Reimbursement Requests noting “Growing Minds Benefit” on the PTA Treasurer’s form (Faculty Gift and Classroom Gifts are eligible with receipt for up to $ 25 reimbursement).
