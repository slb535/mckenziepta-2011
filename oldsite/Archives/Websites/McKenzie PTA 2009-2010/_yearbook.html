It is pretty self-explanatory after that. HOWEVER - if you will do one little thing, it will be SUPER HELPFUL for us when we select photos for the book. So read on:

The above link gets you to the "choose file" page. Once you have chosen everything, click on "upload images." A new screen will appear, and you will be asked for your contact information, in case your advisor Sara needs to contact you. On that screen you will also be asked for the name and grade of the child(ren) in the photo. THIS IS THE SUPER HELPFUL PART: If you know the name of the child(ren) in the photo (and we love the shots with more than one child) please put the first and last name of the first child in the "first name" box, then the first and last names of the remaining children in the "last name" box, separated by commas.

If you don't know the names of any of the children, just type in don't know - but you have to type this in both the "first name" and "last name" boxes.

We look forward to seeing your pictures!
