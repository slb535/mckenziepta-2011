Winter Registration Information

Welcome to the McKenzie Chess Club!  The McKenzie Chess Club is sponsored by the McKenzie PTA.  Chess Club provides our children with the opportunity to study chess in depth while having fun.  Because chess is mathematical and strategic, students master important skills applicable in many academic subjects. Classes are taught by professional Chess Instructors.  Scrimmages or tournament play is optional. The Chess Club is not part of the McClubs after-school program.

Details:

  • Parent participation in Chess Club is voluntary. It is the weekly involvement of parents that provides the foundation of our Club’s success. Your presence alone improves student behavior, encourages them in their efforts and communicates that what they are learning is valuable. Please make every effort to help our students in this richly stimulating endeavor. If you decide volunteering is not an option for you, then an additional $15 fee will be assessed. Please include this fee in your registration.
  • Student Absence and Dismissal - Parents are responsible for notifying Yijia Geohas at yijiaxu@hotmail.com or cell: 773-791-6523 of student absences. Please make every effort to notify Yijia Geohas. Parents are responsible for providing a primary phone number and up to 2 back up phone numbers. If a student is absent from Chess Club and was present for school, you will be notified at the phone numbers provided. 
  • Chess Club ends promptly at 4:15pm in the cafeteria. Adults are required to sign out each student from Chess Club, unless the student was designated as a “walker” on the registration form. Parents may park for a few minutes in the North Parking Lot while picking up students. (Normally, parking is prohibited in the school parking lots.)
  • Parents who are more than ten minutes late picking up their children will be assessed a $10 contribution to the PTA general fund for each 10 minute increment. There are no make-up classes.
  • Student Behavior - All students in the Chess Club are expected to comply with the rules in the McKenzie Citizenship Handbook. Chess Club reserves the right to dismiss students at any time during the current Session without registration fee reimbursement.
