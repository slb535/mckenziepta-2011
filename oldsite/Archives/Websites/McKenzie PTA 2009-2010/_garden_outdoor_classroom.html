Exciting things are happening in the gardens

  • A split rail fence was installed last fall to designate outdoor classroom space.
  • Vegetables were planted by the first thru fourth graders in recycle bins for Earth Week.
  • Two river birch trees were planted in honor of Arbor Day.
  • Our fourth graders are working on their class gift to the school-- planting flowers and grasses along the fence.
  • An arbor and two espaliers will be planted in late May.
  • A gravel path and benches will be installed to create a gathering space for classes in June.

Volunteers are always needed in the garden! Ways you can help out:

  • Volunteer for Outdoor Classroom on Fridays during recess: Two shifts: 11:30 - 12:30 and 12:35 to 1:35
  • Help out on weeding days in the spring and fall
  • Sign up for a week in the summer to be a Garden Keeper -- help keep the gardens watered and weeded.

Please look at calendar and summer garden keeper schedule for days and times. Email Lara Waechtler at lara.waechtler@gmail.com if you can help out.

Summer Maintenance -- Garden Keepers

Each week we need a family or group to help us out with maintaining the garden spaces. This is a great opportunity for boy and girl scout troops as well as families to make a difference and help out their school! What is involved:

  • Watering the gardens 3-4 days per week. If there is rain, then less watering is needed.
  • Weeding the garden as needed. Usually 1 time during the week is sufficient.

Details about water key, hoses, and necessary tools will be provided once you have selected a week. Please email Lara Waechtler at lara.waechtler@gmail.com if interested.

Outdoor Classroom -- Parent Volunteers

Outdoor Classroom is a time when McKenzie students can enjoy the gardens during recess time. Parent volunteers supervise the children as they explore, draw, or work in the gardens. Examples of activities include:

  • Drawing pictures of trees
  • Weeding in the gardens
  • Mixing up the compost
  • Digging up dandelions and planting grass seed
  • Watering different areas
  • Painting pictures
  • Counting insects
  • Looking for worms
  • Identifying plants vs. weeds
  • Identifying insects

At this time, the Outdoor Classroom is limited to Friday recess time. Please refer to the volunteer calendar for days in spring and fall.
