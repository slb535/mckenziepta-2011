Here is McKenzie students’ opportunity to participate in their own reading club!  Our Great Books program for first through fourth graders will begin in January and run for eight weeks.  Parent volunteers will lead discussion groups organized by grade once a week during lunchtime and recess.  The groups will discuss Junior Great Books readings specifically selected to encourage analytical reading and a love of literature.  Student participants are required to come to the discussions having already read (either alone or with a parent) the reading selection at least twice.  The program concludes prior to Spring Break.

The cost is $30 and includes all materials.

Deadline for registration is Friday, November 13th

There are a limited number of spaces per grade.  In the event of more registrants than spaces, a lottery will be held. If your child would like to participate, please register him/her one of the following ways.
