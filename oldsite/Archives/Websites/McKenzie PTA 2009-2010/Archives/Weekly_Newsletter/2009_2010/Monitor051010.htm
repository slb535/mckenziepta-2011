From the Principal

Dear McKenzie Families,

Thanks to the generosity of our wonderful families, our school was filled with special treats throughout this week of Staff Appreciation. Each day we were “showered” with goodies---breakfast, lunch, snacks, flowers, and gift baskets galore! Your thoughtfulness filled both our hearts and our tummies!!

April showers bring May flowers~as seen in our McKenzie gardens.  Thank you to the parents who are bringing our gardens “to life”, along with our fourth grade students, who have turned this service project into their farewell gift to their school.

On Monday morning our students will be practicing bus evacuation drills.  Each fall and spring, our transportation department offers us the opportunity to review the rules and procedures for bus safety.  On Wednesday, our fourth grade students will be touring Highcrest in preparation for their transition to 5th Grade.

Wishing all of our McKenzie Moms a very Happy Mother’s Day!

Sincerely,

Dr. Welter

Reminder Fourth Grade Parents: Your 4th Grader can still join us on our Ride to Highcrest next Wednesday, May 12th at 3:45 pm! Permission slips can be printed out on the McKenzie PTA website.  Please turn them in by Monday, May 10th.  Send your child to school that day with their bike and helmet.  If you would like to ride along, we still need a few parent volunteers.  If you would like to help, email Joan Oh at oreilly@aol.com.  (Rain date Wednesday, May 19th)

Gift Cards: Fundraising while you shop.  This is your last chance for the 2009-2010 school year to order gift cards.  A percentage of every dollar spent on gift cards goes to support the McKenzie PTA efforts.  Order forms can be found on the PTA website and are due back to the PTA Gift Card mailbox by Friday, May 14th.  Thanks so much for your support this year!

The McKenzie Cafeteria is looking for Server and Line Monitor volunteers for the following days in June: Tuesday, 6/1 and 6/8, Wednesday, 6/2, Thursday 6/3 and Friday, 6/4.  Please contact Ann Roberts at akproberts@aol.com if you can help out.  Thanks!

Help in the Garden!  Garden clean up and weeding - Thursday, May 20th at 12:45 pm.  Please come and help, even 20 minutes will make a difference.
