From the Principal

Dear McKenzie Families,

We enjoyed seeing you at Parent-Teacher Conferences this week.  I hope you found the time spent with your child’s teacher to be beneficial. I encourage you to complete the conference evaluation form and return it to school in the coming week.  We use your input to continually improve our practices.

I am happy to announce that two of our teachers, Mrs. Dolkart (1st grade) and Mrs. Weil   (2nd grade) were awarded a Gripp Grant from the District 39 Education Foundation. This will enable them to participate in a 4-day workshop this winter at the nationally acclaimed Center for Teacher and Learning. We look forward to learning from them when they return! The Foundation has funded over 200 grants since 1993, supporting projects that expand, enrich, and complement educational opportunities for our students, staff, and the community in Wilmette District 39.  For more information, please take a look at their website at www.d39foundation.org.

With holidays right around the corner, our PTA Sharing Committee offers us a wonderful opportunity to teach our children the joy of giving rather than receiving.  Through Share the Joy, Share the Warmth, and Share the Feast, McKenzie families can provide for needy families at our sister school in Waukegan, IL. There are many ways to get involved!  Holiday wish lists will be available in our school lobby on Monday through Wednesday of this week. Thank you, in advance, for considering this worthy project!

Don't forget~this month's PTA meeting will be held Tuesday evening at 7:00 p.m. in the library. As part of the meeting, Ms. Wooten and Mrs. Samson will demonstrate ways in which the Smart Board is being used for teaching and learning. We hope you can join us.

Enjoy the rest of this beautiful weekend!

With best regards,

Dr Welter
