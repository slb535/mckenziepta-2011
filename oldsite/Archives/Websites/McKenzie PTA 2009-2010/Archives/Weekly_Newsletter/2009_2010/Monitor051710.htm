From the Principal

Dear McKenzie Families,

As our teachers begin to wrap up final units of the year, students get the chance to culminate their learning in many different ways.  Research projects, writing celebrations, music programs, and Outdoor Education are just some of the ways in which McKenzie students “show what they know”.   What a great year of learning we’ve had!

At Tuesday’s PTA meeting we bid a fond farewell to the 2009-10 Executive Board, thanking them for all of their efforts.  Newly seated members are already hard at work preparing for the new school year.  Be sure to check out the PTA News below for ways in which you can volunteer your time and talents both this year and next.

If you have not yet gone online to register for next year, PLEASE do so now. At this time, only about 25% of McKenzie’s families have registered, making it difficult for us to predict our needs. Some of our grade levels appear to be nearing the point where another section may be needed, however, until we have all registrations completed, we cannot firm up our plans.  Thank you, in advance, for making this a top priority in the coming week!

Wishing you an enjoyable weekend~

Dr. Welter

welterd@wilmette39.org

From the P.T.A.

The 4th Grade Farewell Committee is holding their final planning meeting on Thursday, May 20th from 9 am to 10 am in the Cafeteria.  Any parents of 4th graders who would still like to help with the end-of-the-year festivities are invited to attend.  Any questions, please contact the Committee Co-Chairs Suzanne Day (suzday@yahoo.com) or Lydia Deleo (lcdeleo@sbcglobal.net).

Room Parent Sign Up for 2010/11 School Year:  We are already looking toward next year for Room Parent sign up and we are taking it electronic!  No Backpack Express needed!  Please click here fill in the few fields and hit submit!  It is that easy.  If you have trouble please feel free to email Anne Edmondson @ a.creative@comcast.net with any questions you might have.  

 

Field Day ’10 is on Friday June 4th (Rain Date: Monday June 7th).   Calling all McKenzie Moms and Dads to help! Come watch your child compete in the long jump or 50-yard-dash, and help out with all fun along the way. We can’t have a successful Field Day without YOU! Volunteers are needed to keep score, help with the refreshment table and set up. Sign-ups are in the lobby. If you have questions or would like to help please contact Kelly Rudnicki at kelly@foodallergymama.com or Lew Wint at Lewin.wint@sbcglobal.net. There are two shifts available: Grades 3 and 4: 9:15-11a, and Grades 1 and 2: 1-3p.

Reminder: Fourth Grade Parents: Bike to Highcrest , Wednesday, May 19th at 3:45 pm.  Send your child to school that day with their bike and helmet.  If you have questions, email Joan Oh at oreillyoh@aol.com.

The McKenzie Cafeteria is looking for Server and Line Monitor volunteers for the following days in June: Tuesday, 6/1 and 6/8, Wednesday, 6/2, Thursday 6/3 and Friday, 6/4.  Please contact Ann Roberts at akproberts@aol.com if you can help out.  Thanks!

Spring Recess in the Outdoor Classroom – Volunteers needed 5/14, 5/21, and 5/28.  Spend time with enthusiastic students in the garden.  Attendees choose to draw, paint, explore and work.  Younger siblings welcome.  Please contact Kirsten Stadheim at stadheimwefing@sbcglobal.net if you would like to volunteer.  Shift 1: 11:30-12:45 pm (1st & 3rd grades) Shift 2: 12:30-1:45 pm (2nd & 4th grades) or both.  No gardening experience necessary.  Thank you.
