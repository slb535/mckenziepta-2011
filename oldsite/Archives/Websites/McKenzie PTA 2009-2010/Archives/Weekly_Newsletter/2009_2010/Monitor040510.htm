From the Principal

Dear McKenzie Families,

By now I am sure that many of you are on your way to warmer weather.  I hope you have a wonderful week, enjoying time with family and friends.  We look forward to welcoming back our staff and students on Monday, April 5th.

If you have not yet gone online to sign up for a parent teacher conference, please do so in the coming days/week. If you are unable to attend conferences on either April 15th or 16th, please contact your child’s teacher, and he/she will be happy to schedule a phone conference with you at another time.

Fourth grade parents are reminded to mark their calendars for Wed., April 7th, when we will hold a Parent Information meeting for this year’s Outdoor Education trip (May 26-28).  The meeting will take place in the auditorium from 7:00-8:00 pm.

McKenzie School happily congratulates Mrs. Julie Wade (Kdgn.) and her husband on the birth of their son, Peter John Wade, born on March 21st.  

Happy Spring, everyone!

 From the P.T.A.

Open PTA General Board Positions for 2010-2011.  The PTA is now looking for volunteers for the following positions:

Bike to Highcrest:  coordinate spring bike ride to Highcrest for 4th graders.  Calling all 4th grade parents!  Help us with this fun and informative event.

Kindergarten Enrichment:  work closely with teachers in the planning and coordination of the enrichment curriculum specific to kindergarten.

Family to Family:  serve as a contact for the PTA reaching out to McKenzie families, teachers/staff held in McKenzie cafeteria.

Fall Luncheon:  co-chair needed to coordinate and run coffee held on first day of school when parents can socialize, enjoy light treats and sign up to help at school.

Graphics:  Are you a wiz with computer graphics?   Use your creative spark to help the PTA with our marketing!

Grocery Dollar Delivery: coordinate volunteers and assist in monthly delivery of grocery dollars.

Ice Cream Social: organize and run this outdoor family event held on Sunday afternoon in September, easy and fun.

Last Day Coffee: organize this event held in the cafeteria on the last day of school, coffee and conversation.

McKenzie Monitor Editor: join our fantastic communications team and help in continuing to improve our online newsletter!

School Pictures: Co-chair needed to assist with coordinating Picture Day.

Spring Luncheon: Co-chair needed to help organize annual luncheon held during Teacher/Staff Appreciation week.

Variety Show Producer and Director: now is your chance to be part of something really special to McKenzie and make it big!

Yearbook Orders: handle all aspect of yearbook orders, coordinating with the Yearbook team.

If you are interested in any of the above positions or have any questions, please contact Eleanor Lipinski at eleanor.lipinski@gmail.com.

McKenzie Ladies Night Out !   The ”Pretty in Pink Party” is April 9th.  Please see the Pretty In Pink Invitation for more details

Mark Your Calendars: Movie Fun Night is Friday, April 23rd from 6:30-8:00 pm.

Teacher Appreciation Week will be a fun-filled week for teachers culminating with a luncheon from May 3rd through May 7th.  Room parents will coordinate and solicit volunteers to donate each grade’s “appreciation” for the entire staff.  More information will follow after spring break.  As always, personal notes for teachers are welcomed!

The Teacher Appreciation luncheon hosted by Molly Sullivan and Deanna Goldman will be held Friday, May 7th at the Sullivan’s home located at 1515 Central Ave. (behind McKenzie) during all lunch periods.  Sign ups for food donations will be posted in the front lobby of the school in mid April.   Please reserve the date for this special event and look for more details to come.

Reminder: 2nd Grade Parents: Dental forms are due May 15th.  The forms are available on the District website.  If you have any questions call, or email Kathy Moran at morank@wilmette39.org.

Reminder: Submissions for the Monitor are due by Thursday at 12:00 pm to McKenziePTA@ yahoo.com.  Thanks!
