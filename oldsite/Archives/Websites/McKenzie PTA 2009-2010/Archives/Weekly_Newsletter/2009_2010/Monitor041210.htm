From the Principal

Dear McKenzie Families,

Welcome back from what I hope was a terrific spring break!  Those of us who had a “staycation” enjoyed summer-like weather that reminded us of just how close we are to the end of another school year.  Before you trade in your snow boots for sandals, however, we still have eight weeks of action-packed learning ahead! Please refer to the District 39 Google calendar, as well as your weekly Monitor and classroom newsletters for information on all upcoming events.

Thank you to all the fourth grade parents who were able to attend the Outdoor Education information meeting on Wednesday. If you did not receive your packet, please contact our office immediately.  The deadline for all paperwork is April 30th.  There will be a Parent Orientation for all parents of incoming kindergartners on Thursday, April 22nd, at 6:30 pm in the McKenzie auditorium.  Please help us get the word out to your friends and neighbors who have not yet registered their soon-to-be kindergartner.  Their timely registration will help us anticipate our staffing needs for the fall.

In other news……

**Parents, mark May 5th on your calendars for iLearn ~iTeach, an upcoming parent and community education night in 21st century technology and library media resources. This two-hour event will include two breakout sessions where participants can choose from six different courses.  Instruction and hands-on activities will be provided in each session. For more information including registration links please visit:  http://ilearniteach.wilmette39.org/

**Beginning Jan. 1, 2010 there are new traffic laws concerning driving while using cellular phones and driving while texting in PDF format.  The Wilmette Police Department has started an education campaign and would like to partner with you in getting this information out to all drivers, especially those that are driving in our school zones.  Please note these NEW, IMPORTANT safety laws:

Ø       Texting while driving is unlawful;

Ø       Talking on cell phone while operating a motor vehicle in a school zone is strictly prohibited.

 Please remember that next Thursday is an Early Release, with dismissal at 12:15 pm for Grades 1-4.  There will be no AM Kindergarten class that day.  PM Kindergartners will be in attendance from 8:50-10:20 am.  Parent-teacher conferences will be held on Thursday, from 1:20-8:00 pm, and again on Friday from 8:20-3:15 pm.  I look forward to seeing you then!  

Best regards,

From the P.T.A.

Reminder: Early release day is Thursday, April 15th at 12:15 pm and there is no school on Friday, April 16th.

The PTA’s April Meeting will take place on Tuesday, April 13th at 9:15 am in the McKenzie cafeteria.  This month’s featured speaker will be Principal Dave Palzet from Wilmette Junior High.  He will be speaking about the Wilmette Jr. High experience.

McKenzie Movie Fun Night!  Please join us on Friday, April 23rd for “Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs”.  Children may arrive at 6:20 pm and the film will begin at 6:30 pm.  Parents may pick their children up in the auditorium at 8:00 pm.  Parents are asked to please enter through the stage door.  Ticket order forms have been sent in Backpack Express and should be returned by Wednesday, April 14th.  If you need another form they are available on the PTA Website.  Go to www.mckenzie.com.

McKenzie Movie Night Volunteers needed.  We need volunteers to help before, during or after the movie.  Please indicate your availability on the Movie Night form or contact Faith Kanatzidis at f-kanatzidis@comcast.net.  Thanks!

McKenzie Shopping and Gift Cards:  Don’t forget to use McKenzie Shopping and Gift Cards for your Spring clothing purchases.  We have Kohl’s, Gymboree, Lands End, LL Bean and many more.  April orders are due on Friday the 16th.  More information can be found on the PTA website.  If you have any questions, you can contact Lia Chong at lia38@yahoo.com.

Open PTA General Board Positions for 2010-2011 are still available.  If you are interested a position or have any questions, please contact Eleanor Lipinski at eleanor.lipinski@gmail.com.

Teacher Appreciation Week will be a fun-filled week for teachers culminating with a luncheon from May 3rd through May 7th.  Room parents will coordinate and solicit volunteers to donate each grade’s “appreciation” for the entire staff.  More information will follow after spring break.  As always, personal notes for teachers are welcomed!

The Teacher Appreciation luncheon hosted by Molly Sullivan and Deanna Goldman will be held Friday, May 7th at the Sullivan’s home located at 1515 Central Ave. (behind McKenzie) during all lunch periods.  Sign ups for food donations will be posted in the front lobby of the school in mid April.   Please reserve the date for this special event and look for more details to come.

Reminder: 2nd Grade Parents: Dental forms are due May 15th.  The forms are available on the District website.  If you have any questions call, or email Kathy Moran at morank@wilmette39.org.
