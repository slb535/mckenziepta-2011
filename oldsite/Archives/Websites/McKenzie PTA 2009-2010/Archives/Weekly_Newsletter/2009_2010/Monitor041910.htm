We hope they will spark some lively discussions at home and reinforce all of our efforts to GO GREEN.  For a reminder of each day’s events, please click on the McKenzie PTA link below.  

 

During the month of April we are featuring the Habit of Mind entitled Taking Responsible Risks. We are all sometimes reluctant to experience the unknown, when fear of failure seems to overpower our adventurous spirit.  In school, this can sound like, “If I don’t try it, I won’t be wrong.”  By emphasizing the importance of taking responsible risks, we are teaching our students how to push beyond their comfort zone, stretch their problem-solving abilities, and sustain an overall curiosity about life. On any given school day, this might take place in an academic skill, playing a new game, and/or making a new friend. We want our students to be prepared for the complexities of 21st century, where innovation is prized as much as certainty, and asking the right questions being as important as having the right answers. I hope you will share with your children times in which you took that leap, or risk, and how you felt before, during, and after.  What important lessons we can share!
