From the Principal

Dear McKenzie Families,

Online registration for our fall parent teacher conferences will begin at 10 am on Monday, Oct. 19th. Conferences will be held on Nov. 12th and 13th, with additional time available for kindergarten parents on the morning of Nov. 10th.  We hope you find the new online feature a convenient way to schedule your appointments. 

A big THANK YOU to our Fall Frenzy committee and all its parent volunteers.  I believe we had record-breaking attendance at Friday’s event, with a good time had by all!  Because Friday, the 30th, is an early release day (Gr. 1-4: 12:15, AM Kdgn.: 10:20), our school-wide Halloween parade will begin at 9:15 am.  Our PM Kindergartners are invited to watch or walk in the parade if accompanied by a parent. 

Just as our students continue to learn new things each week, McKenzie offers its parents opportunities to do the same!  Come join the Parent Learning Network on Wednesday evening, for a video lecture and follow up conversation (see below).  Also, mark your calendars for a Family Awareness Network program on October 29th, from 7:00-8:30 pm, at New Trier High School/Winnetka. Howard Gardner, the Harvard Professor best known for his theory of multiple intelligences, will be speaking about his newest book, Five Minds for the Future, and how we can cultivate the "five minds" our children will need to be successful in the 21st century.

As you scroll down through all the weekly happenings, I want to call special attention to the enrichment activities you see posted each week.  All of these wonderful field trips and assemblies are provided to our students through the generosity of our PTA. Your continued support of PTA-sponsored events enables us to offer these wonderful extensions of learning.  Thank you!

We persist in learning how to stick to it, persevere, and keep trying!  Our students are finding examples in their reading and noticing the habit in one another.  Have you called attention to this habit at home?

As always, have a great week, and persist on!

Dr. Welter

From the P.T.A.

Picture Day Retakes-Tuesday, October 27th – 9:00 am-1:00 pm: If your child was absent on Picture Day, order forms are available in the office.  Please be sure your child has his/her photo taken on the 27th so s/he is included in the yearbook and class composite photo.  If you would like your child’s photo retaken, you must return the entire packet of photos at the retake session.  Class composite photos will arrive later in the school year.  Please contact Jill Pinkerton or Genny Wert with questions.

The Boxtop Committee is getting ready to submit boxtops to General Mills on October 28th.  If you have any boxtops at home, please bring them to school and drop them in the designated collection boxes in the main and Kindergarten Lobby.  Last year we raised over $700 for McKenzie and Carman Buckner, and we hope to raise even more this year.  Thanks for your continued support of the program.

Buy Your Tickets for Ladypalooza 2009!    Ticket order forms went home in Friday’s Backpack Express.  FOR THE FIRST TIME at McKenzie, you can buy your tickets online!  Click here www.mckenziepta.com to buy online.

LadyPalooza is coming! It’s November 6th at the Wilmette Golf Club.  Please join us for an evening of shopping, exciting raffle packages and informal modeling, while socializing all in support of McKenzie.  Beverages and appetizers will be served throughout the evening.  

Fall Frenzy: Thank you to the Fall Frenzy Committee for all of its hard work and a job well done!  And a HUGE thank you to every McKenzie parent who worked a shift or baked for the event.  You made the evening a tremendous success.

The PTA Parent Learning Network (PLN) invites you to attend a engaging videotaped lecture this Wednesday, October 21st from 7:00 pm- 9:00 pm at McKenzie School.  Alfie Kohn will be speaking on the topic of Unconditional Parenting.  Join other McKenzie parents and Dr. Welter for an enlightening presentation and an exchange of ideas following the video.  Please RSVP to Julie Johnson at jujohnson007@sbcglobal.net. 

Great Books:  Anyone interested in teaching Junior Great Books during their child’s lunch period for 8 weeks beginning in January, please let Ann Sciortino know at 4Sciortino@gmail.com.  We are in need of teachers this year for this wonderful program!

Kindergarten Chess Club forms will be sent home in Backpack Express this coming week.   Check your backpacks for more information.
