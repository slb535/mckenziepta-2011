From the Principal

Dear McKenzie Families,

This week’s wonderful learning and special Thanksgiving celebrations has put everyone in the holiday spirit. Like you, we are looking forward to an enjoyable week with family and friends.  We are also gearing up for all that December has in store when we return.  Don’t forget to mark your calendar for PTA’s Movie Night on Dec. 11th, Open Gym Night on Dec. 16th, and the First Grade Music Program that is also on the 16th (2:30 pm). 

Thank you to the many families who have contributed to our Sharing Drive for families in need at Carman Buckner School.  Don’t forget that Dec. 2nd is the deadline for your donations.  We are still very much in need of grocery dollar donations, and it’s now easier than ever to contribute through an on-line purchase.  Please check the PTA website for more details.

McKenzie’s 30th Annual Variety Show Kick-Off meeting will be held in the auditorium on Thursday, Dec. 3rd from 7:30-8:30 pm.  I hope you’ll consider signing up to be in the cast or crew, as no prior experience is needed.  If you cannot attend this meeting, please contact Suzanne Armstrong, Karen David, or check out www.mckenziepta.com.

Did you know that this year’s student newspaper, Cheetah Chatter, is soon to be published online? ( http://www.wilmette39.org/~garryj/newspaper/blog/blog.html)

The theme of the first issue is “Family Traditions”.  Students are encouraged to submit an electronic entry, writing about one of their family’s traditions—holidays, vacations, special occasions are all acceptable. All TYPED entries should be submitted to Julie Garry at garryj@wilmette39.org.  Please note that in order to post your child’s writing, we must have the parent permission slip on file.  Happy blogging, everyone!

In classroom news: I am happy to announce that we have two staff members who recently attained National Board Certification—Mrs. Porter and Mrs. Samson. Congratulations to them both!

And lastly, some lunchroom news: we are now serving vanilla soy milk.  The 8.25 oz. carton is not included in the price of a full lunch.  The cost for this is $1.00 per carton.

On behalf of the entire staff, I want to wish you a very Happy Thanksgiving.  We look forward to seeing you back at school on Monday, Nov. 30th.

Best regards,

Dr. Welter

welterd@wilmette39.org

From the P.T.A.

The Sharing Committee: Share the Feast – It’s not too late to donate grocery dollars to the families in need at Carman Buckner School in Waukegan – click on the link below to print out a form or to donate on line.  Share the Warmth – Drop off new hats/mittens in the drop boxes in the lobby and kindergarten area.   Don’t delay – this is the final week – donations accepted through Wednesday 12/2.  Please help make this a happy holiday season for these struggling families!  For more information and to buy grocery dollars click http://www.mckenziepta.com/_sharing.html.  For questions contact Allison Stein ajzstein@yahoo.com.

McKenzie Movie Fun Night!  Please join us on Friday, December 11th for Toy Story.  Children may arrive at 6:20 pm and the film will begin at 6:30 pm.  Parents may pick their children up in the auditorium at 8:00 pm.  Parents are asked to please enter through the stage door.  We are also looking for parent volunteers.  Please indicate on your form if you are available to help.  Ticket order forms have been sent in the Backpack Express and should be returned by Wednesday, December 2nd.  If you need another form they are available on the PTA Website go to www.mckenziepta.com

Mark your calendar: We want to plant the seed so you SAVE THE DATE for the Growing Minds benefit on February 6, 2010.  The Growing Minds Committee is finalizing our collection of silent auction items.  We are seeking high demand items like sports & theater tickets and one of a kind experiences.  Not sure how to dig in?  Consider donating McKenzie Gift Cards to be bundled with other fabulous items.  Visit the Benefit Committee Page on the McKenzie PTA website for donation forms and more Growing Minds details.

Reminder: Open Gym Night is on Wednesday, December 16th, 6:00 pm – 8:00 pm.

Reminder: Submissions for the Monitor are due by 12:00 pm.  Thanks!
