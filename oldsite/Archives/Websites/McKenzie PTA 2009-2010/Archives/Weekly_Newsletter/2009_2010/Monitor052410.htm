From the Principal

Dear McKenzie Families,

At this busy time of the year we are both wrapping up and planning for the year to come.  If you have not yet gone online to register for next year, PLEASE do so now. At this time, less than half of McKenzie’s families have registered for next year, making it difficult for us to predict our needs. It would appear that some of our grade levels are nearing the point where another section may be needed, however, until we have all registrations completed, we cannot firm up our plans. If you have questions about enrollment, registration, and/or the payment of fees, please visit the District 39 website at www.wilmette39.org and click on the link to online student registration. Please take a few extra minutes NOW to help us with LATER.  Thanks!

McKenzie joins LBS Cindy Dougherty and her husband Tim in announcing the birth of their daughter, Victoria Marie, who was born on May 16th.  McKenzie wishes their family much happiness in the days ahead!

4th Grade parents are reminded that Highcrest school supply orders are due on Monday, May 24th.  Also, Outdoor Education is fast approaching!  Don’t forget to bring your child’s necessary medication to the Health Office by Tuesday.  Buses will be ready for boarding at 8:00 am on Wednesday morning, with departure planned for 8:15.  We look forward to three fun-filled days of learning!  Please note that Mrs. Littman will be at the helm in my absence.  

Wishing you an enjoyable weekend with family and friends!

Field Day ’10 is on Friday June 4th (Rain Date: Monday June 7th).   Calling all McKenzie Moms and Dads to help! Come watch your child compete in the long jump or 50-yard-dash, and help out with all fun along the way. We can’t have a successful Field Day without YOU! Volunteers are needed to keep score, help with the refreshment table and set up. Sign-ups are in the lobby. If you have questions or would like to help please contact Kelly Rudnicki at kelly@foodallergymama.com or Lew Wint at Lewin.wint@sbcglobal.net. There are two shifts available: Grades 3 and 4: 9:15-11:00 am, and Grades 1 and 2: 1:00-3:00 pm.

Fall Frenzy Committee 2010: Pumpkins glowing, leaves falling, witches cackling…and McKenzie kids celebrating the fun of Fall Frenzy!  Even though we have yet to enjoy summer, the Fall Frenzy Committee 2010 is now forming-and we’d love to have new faces join in planning this family-oriented October event!  Interested? Please contact Karen Dekker at ckdekker@comcast.net.

The Garden/Outdoor Classroom Committee is looking for volunteers for our last weeding day and the summer.  The last weeding day will be on Friday, June 4—Stop by during Field Day for a little while to help weed the garden areas.  During the summer help out by signing up to be a Garden Keeper for a week (from 6/19-8/22).  This involves 2-3 hours a week of watering and weeding.   Please email Lara Waechtler at lara.waechtler@gmail.com if you are interested in volunteering.

Bravo to our 4th Graders (and their parent volunteers).  Our 4th grade class has given up some of their recess this month to help expand the garden.  They removed the sod, prepared the soil, continued to compost, weeded the garden, mulched the paths and PLANTED the new grasses and perennials along the fence.  Thank You 4th graders!

Gardens/Outdoor Classroom Continue to Grow: Plans are in place to continue the expansion of the gardens.  We are currently looking for a landscape contractor to install permanent paths and benches.   If you know of anyone who might be interested in this project, please email, Kirsten Stadheim at stadheimwefing@sbcglobal.net. Thank you.
