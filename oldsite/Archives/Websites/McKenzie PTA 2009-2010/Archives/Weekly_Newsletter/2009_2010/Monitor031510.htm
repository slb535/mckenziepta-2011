From the Principal

Dear McKenzie Families,

The recent Spring-like weather brings the return of bicycles, scooters, and the like.  Please take a few extra minutes to review with your child the McKenzie safety rules found on the final page of the Citizenship Handbook.  I would like to highlight just a few:

1.     Once your child reaches school property, he/she must dismount the wheeled vehicle, walking it to the bike rack or into the building.

2.     Students must be able to collapse any scooter/razor brought into the building so that it can be safely stored in a locker or hallway.

3.     Helmets must be worn at all times.

Melting snow has left our grounds very muddy.  We have been reminding our students to walk on the sidewalks to prevent tracking dirt throughout our building.  Any support you can give us with this matter will help us to keep our building clean.

When we return from Spring break, the staff will begin creating class lists for next year.  Much time and thought goes into this process.  We review each child’s learning profile and consider a number of factors in creating classroom composites.  Although we feel confident in our ability to appropriately place your child, parents have the opportunity to offer input in the placement decision.  Watch for these forms to come home next week.  The deadline for submission is Friday, March 26th.

I hope you have plans to enjoy the Variety Show in the coming week.  I look forward to seeing you there.  In the meantime, enjoy your weekend!

Best regards,

Dr. Welter
