From the Principal

Dear McKenzie Families,

Students across the state of Illinois will begin the month of March by participating in ISAT testing.  From March 1-10, our students in grades 3 & 4 will be tested in the areas of reading, math, writing, and science.  Each morning, one test is administered, each approximately an hour in length.  Every year our students demonstrate exemplary test taking skills that result in outstanding scores.  Parents can support their child’s efforts by ensuring a good night’s sleep and a healthy breakfast.  Thank you, in advance, for your cooperation.

March 5th is the registration deadline for District 39’s Summer Enrichment Program.  Students who are currently in grades K-7 may enroll in up to three classes.  For more information please scroll down to the bottom of this newsletter.

In backpacks today McKenzie Moms should have found a pink flyer for an upcoming “moms-only” event, Pretty in Pink. A limited amount of tickets go on sale Monday morning.  Don’t miss out (see below).

On Tuesday, March 9th, our monthly PTA meeting will begin at 9:15 in the cafeteria.  I look forward to sharing with you the yearly State of the School Address.

The countdown is on for McKenzie’s 30th Annual Variety Show.  Come find out what all the excitement is about.  I guarantee you won’t be disappointed!

Wishing you an enjoyable weekend,

Dr. Welter

Summer Enrichment Program

District 37/39 five week Summer Enrichment Program offers children completing kindergarten through seventh grade an opportunity for fun, creativity and learning.

The program will be held from June 21, 2010 to July 23, 2010. Children may enroll in one, two or three classes. Bus transportation is available and “link-up” bus service is available after the last morning class each day to students enrolled in Wilmette Park District camps.

March 5, 2010 is the registration deadline until late registration begins March 29th – May 7th. A late fee of $25.00 will be applied after March 29th.

The course catalog containing descriptions of the courses is available at the Mikaelian Education Center, 615 Locust Rd. Wilmette, or at the Wilmette Public Library. The catalog is also available online at the District 39 web site: www.wilmette39.org.

For more information, call the Summer Enrichment Office at 847-256-2450 x 6060.
