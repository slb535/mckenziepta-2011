It’s true!  And many a scholar has shown,
Art can lend heART to wonders unknown.
It’s by helping us see in a different way,
That art, just like science, can help save the day.

And one day the world may be changed, this is true,
By someone who looked from a new point of view.
That’s really the lesson, right down to the core,
It’s simple as that, there isn’t much more.
