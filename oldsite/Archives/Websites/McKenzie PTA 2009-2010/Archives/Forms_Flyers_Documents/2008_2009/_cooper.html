Many parents devote themselves to their child’s happiness, clearing their child’s path of all obstacles and unhappy experiences, and yet recent studies suggest that kids are less happy than ever. What’s the correct approach to securing authentic happiness rather than simply short-term pleasures for kids? Dr. Cooper is an award-winning author, teacher, speaker and psychologist for over thirty years. A graduate of Harvard, Northwestern and Loyola of Chicago, he is affiliated with The Family Institute at Northwestern University. Join us for an informative and thought-provoking lecture. Everyone welcome!
