Helping Hands for Haiti
January 22nd - February 5th
There are many ways to get involved and play a role in McKenzie's Helping Hands for Haiti program.
1) Students can bring in money that they have earned for the Helping Hands for Haiti fund.  They will sign a cut-out hand to post in the lobby area which shows appreciation for their donation.
2) Students can also bring in gently used or new shoes (children's and adult sizes) for the Let's Show Some Sole program.  Drop boxes are set up at each entrance for easy drop off.  The shoes will be donated to Soles4Souls and then shipped to Haiti to help the earthquake victims.
3) Kim & Scott chocolate pretzels will be on sale for $1 after school everyday - funds raised will be donated to the relief efforts.
