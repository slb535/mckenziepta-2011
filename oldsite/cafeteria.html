Cafeteria Facts:

Sign Up: is for one day a week for one whole month

Time Commitment:  11:10am - 1:00pm

Server: Serve lunch to the children, teachers and staff as they come through the line.  The cooks will let volunteers know what they will be serving, how much to serve, etc.  No accompanying infant or toddler siblings are permitted.

Line Monitor: This volunteer will manage the line of children as they wait to be served in the cafeteria, allowing 3 or 4 children in at a time to keep the path between the kitchen and the serving area clear.

For health code reasons, please remember to wear your hair in a ponytail OR a baseball hat, otherwise you may be required to wear a hairnet.

REMINDER: IF YOU ARE UNABLE TO COME ON YOUR DAY, YOU ARE RESPONSIBLE FOR FINDING A SUBSTITUTE! PLEASE CONSULT THE SUB LIST.
