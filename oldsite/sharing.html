During October and November join the Sharing Committee in helping children in need at Carman-Buckner School in Waukegan – only 25 miles away but over 90% of families live below the poverty line.  Here’s how you and your children can help:

 

Donate new hats and mittens in kids sizes.  Collection boxes will be in the lobby until Tuesday November 30th.

 

Help put food on the table for the holidays by purchasing grocery dollars for Carman-Buckner’s neediest families. Pay on-line or drop off a check in the Sharing Committee mailbox (in increments of $25 e.g. $25, $50, $75 etc. payable to McKenzie PTA).  Collection ends on Tuesday November 30th.

Donation Increments

 

Make the holidays bright by purchasing holiday gifts for children at Carman-Buckner.  Include your child in this activity to help teach the spirit of giving for the holidays.  Here’s how it works:

  • Pick up a holiday wish-list before and after school (Monday – Wed, 11/15 – 11/17)
  • Purchase and wrap wish list gifts;
  • Return wrapped gifts after the Thanksgiving break (deadline Tues 11/30). 

If you want to participate but can’t get to school on the wish-list pick-up days, contact Susan Fortier at snsfortier@sbcglobal.net. 

Also join us in delivering the gifts to Carman-Buckner School on Saturday December 4th

 

Contact Susan Fortier with any questions at 251-2165 or snsfortier@sbcglobal.net

 

Email AskCheetah@McKenziePTA.com with any PTA related questions or concerns.
